Five Guys was first opened in 1986 in Arlington County, VA by Jerry and Janie Murrell. Serving hand-formed, grilled burgers and fresh-cut fries cooked in peanut oil, Five Guys remains Washington, DC's favorite burger restaurant. Ingrediants are always served fresh with countless toppings to choose from and free peanuts offered. Today, there are about 1,463 Five Guys restaurants in the United States.

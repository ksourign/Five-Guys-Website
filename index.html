"Start a business or go to college"
-Jerry and Janie Murrell

Named after their five sons, Five Guys was first opened in 1986 in Arlington County, VA by Jerry and Janie Murrell. Serving hand-formed, grilled burgers with peanut oil cooked fries, the restaurant remains Washington, DC's favorite burger restaurant. Ingredients are always served fresh with countless toppings to choose from. We also serve hotdogs and offer free peanuts!

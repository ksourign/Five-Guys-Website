RESTAURANT INFORMATION

"Start a business or go to college" -Jerry and Janie Murrell
Five Guys was first opened in 1986 in Arlington County, VA by Jerry and Janie Murrell. Serving hand-formed, grilled burgers and fresh-cut, peanut oil cooked fries, Five Guys remains Washington, DC's favorite burger restaurant. Ingrediants are always served fresh with countless toppings to choose from and free peanut offerings. Today, there are about 1,463 Five Guys restaurants in the United States.
